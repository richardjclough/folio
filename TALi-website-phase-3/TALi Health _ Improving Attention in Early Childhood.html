Helping children improve their attention.

Attention skills are a part of the fundamental cognitive development in early childhood that supports learning and social development. 

TALi’s pioneering digital therapeutics address the need for early detection and strengthening of attention deficits in early childhood, ages 3 to 8.

 

TALi DETECT

A 20 to 30-minute program comprised of cognitive performance tasks to detect how a child’s attention skills are developing against their peers.

Inattention

Symptoms of inattention are often present as a characteristic of Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD), Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD), Specific Learning Disorder (SLD) or even anxiety and depression.

TALi TRAIN

A 5-week program that provides an enjoyable and measurable way for training children to increase their use of attention skills.

Early Intervention

TALi provides faster access to early interventions for children at the peak of their neuroplasticity.
